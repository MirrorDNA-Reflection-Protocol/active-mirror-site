## What We Are Not

- Not a generic chatbot.
- Not just a memory app.
- Not a design-to-code shortcut.
- Not an always-on agent that acts without consent.
- Not a replacement for every app on day one.
- Not "multi-agent orchestration" as a category. That is becoming infrastructure.

## What We Are

- A private workspace for important work.
- A reflection layer before prediction or action.
- A consent and boundary system for AI help.
- A receipt-backed execution surface.
- A place where memory becomes an approved default, not a hidden assumption.
- A trust/control plane over models, tools, skills, memory, and actions.

## Positioning Guard

Do not lead with "reflection" as a vague abstract noun. Lead with the concrete failure Active Mirror fixes:

```text
AI agents can act, but users cannot yet see, govern, evaluate, reset, or safely remember what happened.
```

Active Mirror answer:

```text
The mirror reflects the work before action, then receipts the action after.
```
