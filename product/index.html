The Onboarding Ritual

We don't ask you to 'chat with your files.' We ask you to frame the sprint:

  • 1. What are you trying to get moving again?
  • 2. What context should I understand?
  • 3. What should I never do or share?
  • 4. What would count as momentum this week?
  • 5. What do you want made first?

Daily Ritual & Proof

Proof Object: The Receipt. We don't hide our reasoning. Every action or generation comes with a receipt explaining exactly why we recommended it based on your context.
